Day 2 - UN Crisis Storymap Challenge

This year we're introducing a new component to the Mapathon, a mapping challenge!

We're partnering with the United Nations Smart Mapping group for this challenge. They will select a package of data for a certain region. Participants will look at the data and select what they want to map.

New mappers are encouraged to participate! Workshops will be held Locally.
